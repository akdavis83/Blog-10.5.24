What else is going on?
Condemnation and attacks will come whether we do the right thing or the wrong thing. Condemnation and attacks will come regardless of whether you believe in Christ or not. It is not possible to control everything that happens to us but even still we must take responsibility to the best of our ability for the situations we find ourselves in. In Christ we are made clean, there is no more condemnation in Him but having been washed we must only wash our feet and take care of our daily walk with the Lord. It is not the external things happening that determine our spiritual state, it is our response, our thoughts, words and actions. Defilement comes from within, purity, virtue and holiness comes from Christ and obedience to His word.
